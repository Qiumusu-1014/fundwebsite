Strategic $500M Investment in Green Hydrogen Infrastructure

DUBAI, UAE — UAE Royal Capital Funding has officially announced a landmark partnership to invest $500 million in developing sustainable green hydrogen infrastructure across the GCC region. This strategic move aligns with the UAE's "Net Zero by 2050" initiative.

The funding will support the construction of two major electrolysis plants and a dedicated export terminal. "This is not just an investment in energy; it is an investment in the future of our planet," stated Sheikh Mohammed bin Hamdan Al Nahyan. The project is expected to create over 2,000 specialized jobs and solidify the UAE's position as a global leader in renewable energy export.

November 15, 2024

Foundation Launches "Future Leaders" Global Education Initiative

ABU DHABI, UAE — In a grand ceremony attended by international dignitaries, the Foundation unveiled the "Future Leaders" scholarship program. This ambitious initiative aims to empower 1,000 students from developing nations over the next five years.

The scholarships will cover full tuition and living expenses for degrees in STEM, International Relations, and Sustainable Development at top-tier universities worldwide. "Education is the bedrock of prosperity. By investing in these brilliant young minds, we are planting seeds for a more peaceful and innovative world," remarked Mr. Youssuf, Secretary General of the Foundation.

Preserving Heritage: New Partnership with UNESCO

PARIS, FRANCE — UAE Royal Capital Funding has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with UNESCO to fund the restoration of historic Islamic art and architecture in the Middle East and North Africa.

The project will focus on digitizing ancient manuscripts and physically restoring three endangered heritage sites. This collaboration highlights the Foundation's deep commitment to cultural preservation and intercultural dialogue. "Our history defines our identity. It is our duty to protect these treasures for future generations," said Sheikh Humaid Al Mualla during the signing ceremony.

Driving the Future: Major Fund Allocated for AI & Tech Startups

DUBAI, UAE — Recognizing the transformative power of technology, the Foundation has launched a specialized venture capital arm dedicated to Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain startups.

With an initial corpus of $100 million, the fund will target early-stage companies in Dubai's Internet City and Silicon Oasis that are developing solutions for smart cities and fintech. This initiative supports the vision of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum to make Dubai the smartest city in the world.

Celebrating 40 Years of UAE-China Diplomatic Relations

BEIJING, CHINA — To commemorate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic ties between China and the UAE, UAE Royal Capital Funding sponsored a high-level economic forum in Beijing.

The forum brought together business leaders and government officials to discuss the "Belt and Road" initiative and expanding bilateral trade. The Foundation emphasized its role as a key facilitator for Chinese enterprises looking to expand into the Arab region, reinforcing the enduring friendship and economic synergy between the two nations.
