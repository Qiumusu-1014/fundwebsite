Dubai RTA Signs Agreement With The Boring Company to Begin Dubai Loop

DUBAI, UAE - Dubai's Roads and Transport Authority signed an agreement with The Boring Company to begin the Dubai Loop, a high-speed underground passenger mobility project.

The pilot stage is expected to include a 6.4 km route with multiple stations, linking high-density areas and testing a new model for fast intra-city movement.

The project strengthens Dubai's broader mobility strategy, which includes autonomous transport, smart infrastructure, air-taxi development and integrated public transit. It also extends the city's relationship with frontier technology companies.

News Watch: For infrastructure investors, Dubai Loop is a signal that the city is willing to test new transport formats when they align with speed, land efficiency and urban competitiveness.

Sources: Government of Dubai Media Office; The Boring Company
