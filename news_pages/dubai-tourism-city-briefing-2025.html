Dubai Tourism Briefing Highlights Sustained Growth and 2026 Destination Roadmap

DUBAI, UAE - Dubai's Department of Economy and Tourism outlined continued momentum in visitor demand, hotel performance and destination development as the emirate prepared its 2026 tourism roadmap.

The briefing emphasized the role of tourism and hospitality in Dubai's wider economic agenda, including investment attraction, city branding and the expansion of high-value visitor segments.

Dubai's tourism model remains closely linked to aviation capacity, retail, events, real estate and cultural infrastructure. For investors, the sector continues to provide signals about consumer demand, international connectivity and long-term urban growth.

News Watch: The visitor economy remains one of Dubai's most visible indicators of confidence, supporting hospitality assets, leisure development and service-sector employment.

Source: Government of Dubai Media Office
