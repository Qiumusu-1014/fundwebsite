Sultan Haitham and UAE President Inspect Joint High-Speed Railway Progress

SOHAR, OMAN — In a display of deep brotherly ties, His Majesty Sultan Haitham bin Tarik and the UAE President conducted a joint inspection of the ongoing UAE-Oman Rail Network project.

The $3 billion project, which will reduce travel time between Sohar and Abu Dhabi to under 100 minutes, is 60% complete. UAE Royal Capital Funding is heavily invested in the developing logistics hubs surrounding the railway terminals.

Our investment strategy focuses on "Green Logistics," ensuring that the warehouses and distribution centers powered by the railway are carbon-neutral. This project signifies a new era of trade velocity for the entire Arabian Peninsula.
