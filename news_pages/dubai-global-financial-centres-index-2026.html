Dubai Climbs to Seventh Place in Global Financial Centres Index

DUBAI, UAE - Dubai recorded its highest-ever position in the Global Financial Centres Index, rising to seventh place globally and consolidating its role as the leading financial hub in the Middle East, Africa and South Asia.

The ranking reflects Dubai's depth in financial services, fintech, professional services, capital markets and regulatory infrastructure. DIFC's continued expansion has also reinforced the city's appeal to asset managers, banks, insurers and technology-led financial platforms.

The result supports Dubai's broader economic strategy by strengthening the city's role as a base for regional headquarters, cross-border investment and global capital flows.

News Watch: A higher financial-centre ranking improves Dubai's credibility with institutional investors and supports the emirate's long-term goal of becoming one of the world's top urban economies.

Sources: Government of Dubai Media Office; DIFC
