Elon Musk Meets Crown Prince Hamdan to Launch "Tesla AI City" in Dubai

DUBAI, UAE — In a move that reshapes the future of urban technology in the Middle East, Tesla CEO Elon Musk met with His Highness Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Crown Prince of Dubai, at Zabeel Palace yesterday.

The high-profile meeting concluded with the announcement of "Tesla AI City", a dedicated district in Dubai South focused on autonomous public transport, humanoid robotics R&D, and sustainable energy grid solutions.

UAE Royal Capital Funding is proud to announce its role as a strategic investment partner in the local infrastructure development for this project. "This collaboration bridges Silicon Valley innovation with Dubai's visionary leadership," stated our Board Member, Sheikh Mohammed bin Hamdan Al Nahyan.

Elon Musk praised the UAE's regulatory agility, stating, "Dubai is one of the few places on Earth where the future is being built today, not just imagined." The project is slated to break ground in early 2026.
