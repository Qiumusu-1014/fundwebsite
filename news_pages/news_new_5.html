Sheikh Mohammed Inaugurates Phase 1 of New Al Maktoum Airport Expansion

DUBAI, UAE - The first phase of the new Al Maktoum International Airport expansion marked a major step in Dubai's long-term aviation and logistics strategy.

The terminal expansion is designed to increase capacity, improve cargo handling and strengthen Dubai South's role as an integrated aviation, trade and logistics corridor.

For investors, the project reinforces Dubai's position as a global connectivity hub, linking air cargo, free zones, logistics warehousing, advanced mobility and regional distribution networks.

News Watch: Airport infrastructure remains central to Dubai's economic model because it supports tourism, high-value trade, e-commerce, freight movement and international headquarters activity.
