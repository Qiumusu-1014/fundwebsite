Sheikh Mohammed Inaugurates Phase 1 of New Al Maktoum Airport Expansion

DUBAI, UAE — His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, officially inaugurated the new terminal wing at Al Maktoum International Airport (DWC) today.

This expansion marks a pivotal moment in Dubai's vision to become the world's aviation capital. The new facility increases cargo capacity by 40% and passenger throughput by 20 million annually.

UAE Royal Capital Funding has played a crucial role as a primary investor in the adjacent "Aerotropolis" logistics corridor. "Infrastructure is the backbone of future trade. Our investment in DWC ensures that Dubai remains the central node of global commerce," said the Fund's spokesperson.

The project features state-of-the-art automated logistics systems, which align with the Foundation's strategy to support smart infrastructure and sustainable supply chain solutions.
