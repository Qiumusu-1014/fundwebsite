Dubai Hosts Virtual Tourism Summit Connecting 250 Global Travel Leaders

Dubai is serving as the hub for Engage by Elevate, a virtual tourism summit linking more than 250 hotels, destination partners, travel agents and tour operators across global markets.

Dubai tourism hospitality travel Elevate World MICE destination partners Middle East

DUBAI, UAE - Dubai is hosting Engage by Elevate, a three-day virtual tourism summit organised by UAE-based travel and tourism company Elevate World from June 2 to June 4, 2026.

According to reports attributed to Emirates News Agency, the summit brings together more than 250 senior industry leaders, hotel groups, destination partners, travel agents and tour operators. Elevate World's Dubai headquarters is serving as the event hub, while international buyers are participating virtually from global markets.

The programme is designed to support planning across the UAE and wider Middle East visitor economy, with registered participants discussing destination readiness, trade partnerships, hotel demand and new opportunities for the final quarter of 2026.

Aletihad reported that destination partners linked to the summit include Dubai Department of Economy and Tourism, Abu Dhabi Tourism, Miral, Ras Al Khaimah Tourism Development and Wynn Al Marjan Island. The format includes scheduled one-to-one meetings, destination updates and sector-focused industry sessions.

For business and investment observers, the summit reinforces Dubai's role as a regional convening platform for tourism, hospitality, MICE travel and destination partnerships. It also highlights the continued use of hybrid and virtual formats to connect Middle East suppliers with international travel buyers efficiently.

News Watch: Tourism remains a high-visibility driver of Dubai's service economy, supporting hospitality assets, aviation connectivity, events, retail and long-term destination development.
