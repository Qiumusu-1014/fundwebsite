Doha Royal Summit: Emir of Qatar Hosts GCC Leaders on Economic Unity

DOHA, QATAR — His Highness Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani convened a historic summit of GCC leaders in Doha yesterday to accelerate the timeline for a unified Gulf digital economic framework.

The summit focused on reducing trade barriers and integrating digital banking systems across member states. UAE Royal Capital Funding was honored to be invited to the exclusive financial roundtable, advising on private sector integration.

"The unity of the Gulf is our greatest economic asset," His Highness stated. The Foundation has pledged to support cross-border startups that leverage this new economic corridor, specifically between Doha and Dubai, aiming to foster a seamless digital marketplace by 2030.
