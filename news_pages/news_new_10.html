Altérra Fund Deploys $5B for Climate Projects in Southeast Asia

SINGAPORE — Altérra, the UAE's $30 billion climate investment vehicle launched at COP28, has announced its first major deployment in Southeast Asia. The fund is allocating $5 billion towards renewable energy grids and decarbonization technologies in Indonesia, Vietnam, and the Philippines.

UAE Royal Capital Funding is co-investing alongside Altérra, focusing on the high-tech manufacturing components of solar panels and wind turbines.

This massive injection of capital aims to mobilize private finance to help emerging economies meet their Net Zero targets. "Climate finance is the greatest investment opportunity of our generation," stated the CEO of Altérra. "By bridging the gap in Asia, we are securing a sustainable future for the planet while generating robust returns for our stakeholders."
