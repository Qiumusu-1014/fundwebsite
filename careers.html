Global Talent Recruitment 2026

Join the UAE Royal Foundation for Friendship in Dubai to advance research on global strategy, regional development, international cooperation and global governance.

Why Join the Foundation?

Rooted in the Gulf region and connected to global intellectual networks, the UAE Royal Foundation for Friendship is committed to delivering high-quality intellectual support on global strategic issues, international cooperation, regional development and global governance through independent research and cross-domain resource integration.

Competitive Global Package

Salary aligned with leading international think tanks, including base salary, research subsidies, project bonuses and year-end incentives.

Research & Global Access

Dedicated research funding and direct access to academic forums, field research, international cooperation and high-level global networks.

Flexible Collaboration

Flexible remote or part-time working models, supported by a professional administrative team, plus visa and medical insurance for eligible candidates.

Open Positions

Honorary Director

Dubai, UAE Global Recruitment 15+ Years

Provide top-level intellectual support for the Foundation's development and help shape its strategic academic and international influence.

Responsibilities

  • Provide insights and recommendations on the global political and economic landscape and regional cooperation trends.
  • Participate in planning and guiding major academic conferences and international forums.
  • Connect global high-end academic resources and government networks to deepen cooperation with think tanks, international organizations and government agencies.
  • Supervise research quality and academic norms to ensure professionalism and authority in the Foundation's intellectual outputs.

Requirements

  • More than 15 years of senior government management experience or experience as a senior scholar at a world-renowned university or research institution.
  • Broad reputation in international politics, international relations, global economy or related fields.
  • Strong strategic vision, cross-cultural communication skills, and familiarity with global governance systems and regional development dynamics.
  • Preference for candidates who have led or deeply participated in major policy formulation or international cooperation projects.
  • Commitment to academic integrity, neutrality and social responsibility, with willingness to support the Foundation's development.
  • Excellent written and spoken English, Arabic and Persian, with the ability to clearly present complex research findings.

Policy Advisor

Dubai, UAE Middle East & Global Policy 10+ Years

Lead in-depth policy research on key Middle East and international issues and translate research into actionable analysis for partners and decision-makers.

Responsibilities

  • Research political and economic policy, cross-regional cooperation mechanisms and geopolitical risks in the Middle East and beyond.
  • Prepare high-quality policy briefings and thematic reports that provide accurate analysis and decision-making references.
  • Participate in major project risk assessment and feasibility studies, forecasting implementation effects and potential policy risks.
  • Join academic seminars and policy dialogue activities to disseminate research outcomes and strengthen policy communication.

Requirements

  • More than 10 years of policy formulation experience in government departments, international organizations or think tanks, or experience as a professor or senior researcher at a well-known university.
  • Strong achievements in relevant research and policy fields.
  • Deep understanding of the global political economy and the policy environment and development needs of the Middle East and surrounding regions.
  • Excellent written and spoken English, Arabic and Persian, with the ability to clearly present complex research findings.

Senior Research Fellow

Dubai, UAE Research & Analysis 5+ Years

Conduct high-value thematic research with practical relevance across economic cooperation, governance, public policy and risk assessment.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct thematic research in economic cooperation, political governance, public policy and risk assessment, producing results with practical guiding significance.
  • Compile high-quality policy briefings and thematic reports for the Foundation's partners.
  • Track global policy dynamics and academic frontiers, build research databases and support the Foundation's research system.
  • Participate in academic seminars and policy dialogues, and support policy advisors on major research topics.

Requirements

  • More than 5 years of experience in government policy research institutions, renowned think tanks or well-known universities.
  • Published high-level academic achievements or policy reports in relevant target fields.
  • Solid theoretical foundation, systematic research methods, and proficiency in policy analysis and risk assessment models.
  • Ability to independently carry out interdisciplinary and cross-domain thematic research.
  • In-depth research expertise on the political economy of the Middle East and international cooperation mechanisms.
  • Excellent written and spoken English, Arabic and Persian, with the ability to clearly present complex research findings.
