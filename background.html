Background Overview

Origin, Vision, and Future

Origin & Establishment

UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment was initiated in September 2020 by more than ten ruling royal family members, including Sheikh Humaid Al Mualla, Sheikh Mohammed bin Hamdan Al Nahyan, and Sheikh Hamdan bin Ahmed Al Maktoum.

After several years of preparation, the Foundation was officially established in 2024, coinciding with the 40th anniversary of diplomatic relations between China and the UAE.

Membership

Today, the Foundation consists of over 60 royal members from the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain, Yemen, and Qatar.

Foundation Introduction

UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment works closely with businesses, government entities, and international partners to identify high-quality and sustainable projects, creating a favorable investment environment across the Arab region.

The Foundation aims to support the UAE in developing an innovative, sustainable, and globally competitive economic ecosystem that enables enterprises to grow and prosper.

Team and Development

We continue to welcome new royal members and partners, conducting in-depth market research and rigorous project evaluation to ensure value and profitability.

Our experienced advisory team will conduct thorough due diligence and provide strategic and operational solutions to achieve optimal results.

Vision and Mission

UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment is committed to advancing regional and global sustainable development through multi-sector investments and philanthropic efforts.

Our Mission Includes:
  • Generating long-term value through high-quality investments.
  • Opening new business opportunities that meet our investment criteria.
  • Leveraging regional networks to support strategic projects.
  • Promoting economic growth, social progress, technological innovation, and cross-cultural understanding.

We welcome partners worldwide to collaborate with us in achieving prosperity and development.
