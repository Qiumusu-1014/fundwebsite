About Us

Foundation Introduction

UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment was initiated in September 2020 by more than ten ruling royal family members, including Sheikh Humaid Al Mualla, Sheikh Mohammed bin Hamdan Al Nahyan, and Sheikh Hamdan bin Ahmed Al Maktoum.

Today, the Foundation consists of over 60 royal members from the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain, Yemen, and Qatar. UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment works closely with businesses, government entities, and international partners to identify high-quality and sustainable projects, creating a favorable investment environment across the Arab region.

Team and Development

We continue to welcome new royal members and partners, conducting in-depth market research and rigorous project evaluation to ensure value and profitability.

Our experienced advisory team will conduct thorough due diligence and provide strategic and operational solutions to achieve optimal results.
