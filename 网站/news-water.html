UAE Royal Capital Announces New Clean Water Initiative in Sudan

October 24, 2023  |  By Media Team

Abu Dhabi, UAE – UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment Co.LLC is proud to announce the launch of a comprehensive $5 million fund dedicated to establishing sustainable water infrastructure in rural regions of Sudan.

Access to clean water remains one of the most critical challenges in the region. This initiative, dubbed "Project Blue Life," aims to construct 50 solar-powered water filtration stations over the next 18 months.

Sustainable Impact

Unlike temporary aid solutions, these stations are designed to be maintained by local community committees. "Our goal is not just to provide water, but to empower communities to manage their own resources," stated H.E. Ahmed Al-Mansouri, Chairman of the Foundation.

The project is expected to serve over 120,000 residents, significantly reducing waterborne diseases and freeing up time for children—especially girls—to attend school instead of walking miles to fetch water.
