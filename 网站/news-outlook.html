2024 Investment Outlook: The Rise of Ethical Finance

August 10, 2023

Our Chief Investment Officer, Omar Fatima, has released the foundation's annual "Investment Outlook Report" for the upcoming fiscal year. The report highlights a significant shift in global capital towards Sharia-compliant and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investments.

Key Findings

  • Green Bonds: Demand for green bonds in the MENA region has tripled in the last 24 months.
  • Tech for Good: High-yield opportunities are emerging in EdTech startups focused on developing regions.
  • Stability: Ethical funds have shown greater resilience to market volatility compared to traditional speculative assets.

The full report is available for download in our Publications section.
